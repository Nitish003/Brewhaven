THE BEGINNING

We didn't start a brand.
We started a conversation.

In 2021, two friends sat in a small café in Coorg, nursing cups of filter coffee made from beans they'd just watched being handpicked on a farm twenty minutes away. The difference was undeniable. It was not just coffee. It was a place, a morning, a person.

Three years and forty farm visits later, The Brew Haven exists to close that gap between the person who grew your coffee and the person who drinks it. We believe that transparency is the most powerful ingredient in specialty coffee — more than elevation, more than variety, more than process.

We source exclusively from farms we have visited, from farmers we know by name. Every bag you receive carries with it the coordinates of where it was grown, the name of the estate, and a note from the farmer who picked it.

OUR JOURNEY

From Idea to Origin

2021

The First Cup

Two travel writers visit Coorg on an unrelated assignment. They drink coffee on a farm. They never fully return to the life they had before that morning.

2022

The Farm Visits Begin

Over 12 months, 40 farm visits across Coorg and Chikmagalur. Relationships built. Direct trade agreements signed. The first harvest purchased.

2022

The Roastery Opens

A small-batch roastery established in Bangalore with zero-waste commitment. The first official Brew Haven roast is produced — the Coorg Light.

2023

Launch & First 100 Customers

The Brew Haven launches online. Within 30 days, the founding batch of 100 customers receives their first bags. The reviews are overwhelming.

2024

Expanding the Collection

Dark Reserve Blend, Honey Process, Espresso Blend, and the South Indian Filter Blend join the collection. The Connoisseur's Box subscription launches.

2025

Carbon-Offset Logistics

Every shipment becomes 100% carbon-offset through a reforestation program in the Western Ghats. We partner with three more estates.

2026

500+ Happy Customers & Growing

Over 500 customers. 8 products. 2 origins. And still, each bag is packed by hand and accompanied by a handwritten note from our team in Bangalore.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our Values

Radical

Transparency

Every bag shows the farm name, GPS coordinates, elevation, process method, and harvest date. We hide nothing about where your coffee comes from.

Direct

Trade Always

No brokers, no middlemen. We buy directly from every farm at a premium above fair trade rates. Our farmers are paid before we bottle a single drop.

Small

Batch Roasting

We never roast in bulk. Every production run is 5–15kg maximum. Freshness is never compromised. Your bag is roasted within days of shipping.

Zero

Waste Roastery

Coffee chaff is composted and returned to farm partners. Our packaging is 100% plant-based and compostable. Waste is unacceptable to us.
