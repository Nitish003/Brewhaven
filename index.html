OUR STORY

Born in the
Mist of Coorg

Deep in the Western Ghats, where fog clings to ancient arabica trees at dawn, The Brew Haven was born. We didn't set out to start a coffee brand. We set out to tell the truth — about where your coffee comes from, who grows it, and what it cost the earth to reach your cup.

We partner directly with small family farms in Coorg and Chikmagalur, cutting out brokers entirely. Every bean is paid for at a fair premium above market rate. Because great coffee isn't an accident — it's a covenant between the roaster, the farmer, and the land.

From the rain-shadowed estates of Chikmagalur's Bababudangiri hills to the pepper-vine-shaded coffee groves of Kodagu, we source with reverence. Because the story of Indian single-origin coffee deserves to be told loudly.

Kodagu, Karnataka

Grown at 1,200m altitude in Kodagu's misty, pepper-vine-shaded estates. Handpicked during peak harvest, sun-dried on raised beds. The high altitude and cool nights amplify inherent sweetness — producing a clean, bright cup with floral delicacy.

ORIGIN STORY

Chikmagalur, Karnataka

Sourced from the Bababudangiri hills — where Indian coffee history was born. This reserve blend is slow-roasted in small batches to develop deep, resonant complexity. Both Robusta and Arabica are represented, making this a bold, structured cup for the discerning palate.

Estate: Mullayanagiri Hills · Elevation: 1,800m

OUR COMMITMENT

We Owe It to the Earth

100%

Biodegradable Packaging

All our bags are made from plant-based, compostable materials. Your cup of coffee shouldn't cost the earth a plastic debt.

30%+

Direct Trade Pricing

We pay farmers 30% above fair trade price. Because the person who grew your coffee deserves more than market rates.

Zero

Zero-Waste Roastery

Our roastery runs on green energy. Coffee chaff is composted. Silver skin is repurposed. We believe waste is just poor imagination.

CO₂

Carbon-Offset Delivery

Every shipment is 100% carbon-offset through verified reforestation programs in the Western Ghats. Your delivery plants a tree.
